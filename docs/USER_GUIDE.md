# User Guide - BestMealMate

Welcome to BestMealMate! This guide will help you get the most out of your family meal planning experience.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit [bestmealmate.com](https://www.bestmealmate.com)
2. Click **Get Started Free**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Check your email and click the confirmation link
5. You're in!

### The Onboarding Flow

When you first sign up, we'll walk you through a quick setup:

1. **Tell us about your challenges** - We understand meal planning is hard!
2. **Create your household** - Give your family a name
3. **Add family members** - Who eats dinner together?
4. **Set dietary needs** - Allergies, restrictions, and preferences for each person

## Features Guide

### Meal Plan (Calendar)

Your weekly meal planner:

**Adding Meals:**
1. Click on any day's meal slot
2. Browse or search recipes
3. Click **Add to Plan**

**Rearranging:**
- Drag and drop meals between days
- Click **Swap** to exchange two meals

**Marking Complete:**
- Click the checkmark when you've cooked a meal
- Skip meals you didn't make (helps AI learn!)

### Pantry

Track everything in your kitchen:

**Adding Items:**
1. Click **Add Item**
2. Type the ingredient name (we'll suggest matches)
3. Enter quantity and select location (fridge/freezer/pantry)
4. Set expiry date (important for AI suggestions!)
5. Click **Add**

**Locations:**
- **Fridge** - Refrigerated items
- **Freezer** - Frozen items
- **Pantry** - Shelf-stable goods
- **Spice Rack** - Spices and seasonings

**Expiry Tracking:**
- **Green** - Fresh (more than 3 days)
- **Yellow** - Expiring soon (1-3 days)
- **Red** - Expired

**Staple Items:**
Mark items as "staples" (milk, eggs, butter). We won't add these to your grocery list automatically.

### Grocery List

Smart shopping made easy:

**Auto-Generated Lists:**
When you plan meals, we automatically add missing ingredients.

**Manual Items:**
Click **Add Item** to add anything else you need.

**Shopping Mode:**
1. Click **Start Shopping**
2. Check off items as you buy them
3. Items organize by aisle

**Sharing:**
Share your list with family members so anyone can shop.

### Recipes

Browse and save recipes:

**Finding Recipes:**
- Search by name
- Filter by meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
- Filter by difficulty (easy, medium, hard)
- Toggle **Quick Meals** for under 30 minutes
- Toggle **Kid Friendly** for picky eaters

**Saving Favorites:**
Click the heart icon to save recipes to your favorites.

**Adding to Plan:**
Click the **+** button to add any recipe to your meal plan.

**Creating Your Own:**
Click **Add Recipe** to enter your family's favorites.

### Family

Manage who you're cooking for:

**Adding Members:**
1. Click **Add Member**
2. Enter name and age
3. Select role (admin, member, child)
4. Set dietary restrictions
5. Add any allergies (with severity)
6. List food dislikes
7. Mark as "picky eater" if applicable

**Dietary Restrictions:**
- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Gluten Free
- Dairy Free
- Keto
- Paleo
- Halal
- Kosher
- Low Sodium
- Low Sugar

**Allergies:**
We take allergies seriously! The AI Chef will NEVER suggest meals containing allergens. Severity levels:
- **Mild** - Causes discomfort
- **Moderate** - Significant reaction
- **Severe** - Life-threatening

### AI Chef

Your personal cooking assistant:

**Ask Anything:**
- "What can I make for dinner tonight?"
- "I have 20 minutes, what's fast?"
- "Something the kids will eat"
- "Use up my chicken before it expires"

**Smart Suggestions:**
The AI considers:
- What's in your pantry
- What's expiring soon
- Everyone's dietary needs
- What you've eaten recently (for variety)
- How much time you have

### Settings

Customize your experience:

**Household Settings:**
- Household name
- Timezone
- Preferred grocery store

**Notifications:**
- Expiry reminders (when food is about to expire)
- Meal reminders (what to cook today)
- Grocery reminders (time to shop)
- Email and push notification preferences

**Subscription:**
- View current plan
- Upgrade to Premium or Family
- Manage billing

## Tips & Tricks

### Get Better AI Suggestions

1. **Keep your pantry updated** - Remove items when you use them
2. **Set expiry dates** - AI prioritizes expiring ingredients
3. **Complete dietary profiles** - The more we know, the better we suggest
4. **Rate meals** - Tell us what you liked!

### Save Time

1. **Batch cook on Sundays** - Plan ahead for busy weekdays
2. **Use the grocery list** - Never forget an ingredient
3. **Set up staples** - Common items won't clutter your list

### Reduce Food Waste

1. **Check Expiring Soon** - Use these first!
2. **Plan around what you have** - Ask AI Chef for ideas
3. **Freeze before expiring** - Update location in pantry

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can multiple people access the same household?**
A: Yes! On Premium and Family plans, multiple family members can have their own logins and access the shared household.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings > Subscription > Manage Subscription. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Absolutely. We never share your personal information or meal data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I import recipes from websites?**
A: Not yet, but it's on our roadmap! For now, you can manually add recipes.

**Q: How does the AI handle allergies?**
A: Very seriously. The AI will never suggest a meal containing any allergen marked in a family member's profile.

**Q: Can I use BestMealMate on my phone?**
A: Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers.
