## InVideo AI Prompt

```

Target Audience: Busy parents, families with dietary restrictions, health-conscious households.

SCRIPT:

[Scene 1 - 0:00-0:08]
(Show stressed parent looking at empty fridge, then phone notification)
VOICEOVER: "It's 5pm. You're exhausted. And the dreaded question hits: What's for dinner?"

[Scene 2 - 0:08-0:15]
(Show app interface, family profiles being set up)
VOICEOVER: "Meet BestMealMate. Tell us about your family - allergies, preferences, dietary needs."

[Scene 3 - 0:15-0:23]
(Show AI generating meal suggestions, calendar view)
VOICEOVER: "Our AI instantly creates personalized meal plans that work for everyone. Keto for dad? Nut-free for the kids? Done."

[Scene 4 - 0:23-0:32]
(Show phone scanning fridge, pantry items appearing)
VOICEOVER: "Snap a photo of your fridge. AI identifies every item and prioritizes what's expiring first."

[Scene 5 - 0:32-0:40]
(Show grocery list being auto-generated, organized by aisle)
VOICEOVER: "Smart grocery lists write themselves. Organized by aisle. Never buy what you already have."

[Scene 6 - 0:40-0:48]
(Show family cooking together, happy dinner scene)
VOICEOVER: "Less stress. Less waste. More time enjoying meals with your family."

[Scene 7 - 0:48-0:55]
(Show app stats: 50K families, 40% less food waste)
VOICEOVER: "Join 50,000 families who've saved time, money, and sanity."

[Scene 8 - 0:55-1:00]
(Show BestMealMate logo, call to action)
VOICEOVER: "BestMealMate. Start free today."
TEXT ON SCREEN: "bestmealmate.com - Start Planning Free"

MUSIC: Uplifting, modern acoustic with light electronic elements. Optimistic but not cheesy.

INCLUDE: App screenshots/mockups, diverse family representations, food preparation footage, smartphone interactions.
```

## How to Use with InVideo AI

1. Go to https://invideo.io/ai
2. Sign in or create free account
3. Click "Create Video"
4. Select video type (YouTube, Social, etc.)
5. Paste the prompt above
6. Choose aspect ratio (16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok)
7. Let AI generate the video
8. Edit scenes, add your logo, adjust voiceover
9. Export and publish!

## Tips for Best Results

- Upload your actual app screenshots when editing
- Add your logo in the final scene
- Adjust voice speed if needed
- Preview on mobile before publishing
- Add captions for accessibility
